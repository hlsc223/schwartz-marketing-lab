### Schwartz Marketing Lab Launches to Help B2B Companies Stay Visible as AI Overviews Erase the Majority of Organic Search Clicks

**Founder Quinn Schwartz built the company to help B2B brands get found in AI search, Google, and modern discovery as ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google's AI Overviews replace traditional search results with AI-generated answers.**

PORTLAND, Ore. — June 23, 2026 — Schwartz Marketing Lab (S.LAB), founded by Quinn Schwartz, today announced its official launch to help B2B companies get found in AI search, Google, and modern discovery as organic search traffic erodes under the weight of AI-generated answers. The company combines SEO & AEO (including generative engine optimization, or GEO), content systems, PR & media, and executive authority into a single program built for how buyers now research vendors — through ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google's Gemini, and AI Overviews, not search results pages alone.

The shift is well documented. Pew Research Center, tracking 68,000 real search queries, found that users clicked a result only 8% of the time when an AI-generated summary appeared, versus 15% when it did not — nearly half the rate. SparkToro estimates fewer than one in three Google searches now ends in a click to an external site, and Ahrefs has reported click-through rates for top-ranking pages falling by as much as 58% when an AI Overview appears above them. For many B2B companies, the result is declining organic traffic despite continued investment in search visibility.

Schwartz Marketing Lab was built for that shift specifically. Its program spans four connected service areas:

- **SEO & AEO** — technical and content optimization for traditional search engines (SEO) and for AI-generated answers and citations (AEO), including generative engine optimization (GEO) for AI Overviews and chat-based search
- **Content systems** — editorial strategy and content production
- **PR & media** — earned coverage and journalist relationships that build third-party credibility
- **Executive authority** — helping founders and executives build visibility and credibility through thought leadership content, media opportunities, and expert commentary

Most agencies specialize in one of these disciplines. Schwartz Marketing Lab treats all four as one connected program rather than separate services handled by separate vendors.

"We didn't start this company to chase rankings," said Quinn Schwartz, founder of Schwartz Marketing Lab. "Rankings still matter, but they're no longer the finish line. The real question now is whether ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google's AI Overviews even know your company exists, and whether you're consistently cited, referenced, or surfaced when buyers ask the questions that matter to your business. That takes evidence, clear positioning, and real authority — not keyword density."
