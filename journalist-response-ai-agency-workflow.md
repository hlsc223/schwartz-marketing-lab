# Response — "Building an AI-Powered Agency Workflow Without Replacing Strategic Human Expertise"

**Tool/workflow:** Profound, an AI visibility platform that tracks how a brand shows up across ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google's AI Overviews — what we're being cited for, what we're missing, and which communities are actually feeding those citations.

**The agency problem it solved:** As an SEO agency, most of our clients come to us scared about falling traffic, and a lot of that is real AI cannibalization — AI Overviews and chat-based search are absorbing clicks that used to land on their site. But the conversation that actually matters isn't "how do we get the old traffic back." It's reframing what's happening: the content that performed well in traditional search is often still performing — it's just being read, summarized, and cited inside an AI answer instead of clicked. Profound let us show clients that distinction directly instead of arguing it.

**How we implemented it:** The hardest part of any AI visibility tool isn't the tool — it's knowing which prompts to track. Profound surfaces prompt volume, but we don't rely on that alone. We take traditional SEO keyword data and convert it into actual buyer language by pulling from Reddit threads, review sites, and real sales call transcripts — the way people actually phrase questions when they're not thinking in keywords. Those become the prompts we track, because that's what someone's actually typing into ChatGPT or Perplexity, not what a keyword tool assumes they're typing.

**Measurable outcome:** Running this for Nooks, an AI-powered sales dialer, we increased their share of voice by 43% and their citation rate by 37% in two months.

**One caution:** Don't get obsessed with AEO to the point you neglect the fundamentals. It's a genuinely exciting discipline, but traditional SEO hygiene still matters — technical health, site structure, the basics. If you chase listicle content optimized for AI citations and let your core SEO fall apart, every part of your visibility suffers, AI included.
